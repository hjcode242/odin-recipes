Blueberry Compote

This sweet blueberry compote makes a delicious and beautiful topping for hot pancakes.

Recipe by: Hannah-Joy Simms



Recipe Description




Prep Time:       Cook Time:       Total Time:
5 minutes          25 minutes          30 minutes

Servings:
12


Jump to Nutrition Facts

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen blueberries, unthawed, divided
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup water

Directions


Step 1

Place 1/2 of the blueberries into a small, heavy saucepan with sugar and water;
bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer, stirring often, until blueberries burst,
about 10 minutes.

Step 2

Stir remaining blueberries into the saucepan; continue to simmer 
until compote thickens and coats the back of a spoon, about 8 minutes.
